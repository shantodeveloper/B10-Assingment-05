As-Sunnah Foundation Mosque Complex

As-Sunnah Foundation Mosque Complex will be the focal point of As-Sunnah Foundation's multifaceted activities for the welfare of the country, nation and Ummah. In-sha-Allah, the complex will house a model mosque and a modern madrassa with an integrated curriculum to produce Islamic scholars fit for the needs of the age.

Emergency Flood Fund

About 26,000 square kilometers (18%) of Bangladesh are flooded every year during the monsoon season. At this time, especially the Banvasi people of the northern region of the country were subjected to extreme suffering. As Sunnah Foundation undertakes relief activities every year in the flood-affected districts of the country and stands beside the distressed and helpless people.
